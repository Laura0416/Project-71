This sports stadium was added so we can make the viewing experience of sports better for people and to add a new place where people can practice their sports.

was added in winter of 2017

This gallery was added to show newcomers our history and what we can provide for our students. This gallery is also a place where people can take a break, relax, and look at the sculptures and paintings of our past.

library

was added in spring of 2010

This library was added to give our students a wider variety of knowledge. This library gives our students a place to study, relax, and learn something new.
